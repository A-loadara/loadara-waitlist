The Problem

Most dump truck businesses are still stitched together from texts, paper, and spreadsheets.

📱

Operations live in texts and calls

Jobs, truck assignments, and day to day changes get coordinated through calls, texts, and group chats. One missed message can mean one missed load.

💸

You work today and get paid months later

Cash keeps going out for fuel, tires, payroll, and insurance while billing gets delayed.

📄

Lost tickets become lost revenue

Paper tickets disappear, loads go unrecorded, and you cannot bill cleanly for work you can’t prove happened.

🧩

Your business is scattered across tools

Dispatch in one place, invoices in another, compliance somewhere else, and the rest in spreadsheets. You are stitching together the business every day.

Operating System

Operations and crew coordination

Step 3

Revenue visibility built in

Turn completed work into invoices, track payments and receivables, and keep a clean trail from job to cash collected.
